     2. Thiruvaiyaru Festival

      Date: 15 January

     Thiruvaiyaru is a famous music festival that is celebrated in Thanjavur in Tamil Nadu.
     On Pushya Bahula Panchami day, Saint Thyagaraja attained Samadhi and so this festival is celebrated every year on this day.
     Every year in the month of January, a lot of composers come to Thanjavur for attending the festivities of this grand festival.


     3. Thaipusam

      Date: 28th Jan

     Thaipusam is celebrated on the full moon day of the Thai month in the Tamil Calendar in Tamil Nadu.
     The younger son of Lord Shiva was born on this day. His name is Lord Subramaniam. People take vows and keep them.
     They believe that if they would take vows in front of Lord Subramaniam, he would ensure that they keep their vows forever.


     4. Karthigai Deepam

      Date: 10th Dec

     Karthigai Deepam is the festival of lights which marks the beginning of good vibes and positivity in our life.
     The Tamil calendar plays an important part in understanding the various festivals of Tamil Nadu.
     Karthigai, the month in the Tamil calendar that is in November and December, this festival is celebrated.
